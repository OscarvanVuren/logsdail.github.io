Welcome

Hi! You've reached the group pages of Dr. Andrew Logsdail. We're interested in Computational Chemistry and based in the Cardiff Catalysis Institute at Cardiff University.

Here you will find information about who we are, what we do and how we do it. Please have a browse and ask questions!
